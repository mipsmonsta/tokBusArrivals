Use your phone to find the nearby bus stops. To enable this feature, remember to allow the "location" permission when using the app.

Your current location will be compared to the 5000+ bus stop coordiates island-wide to find the nearest one.

Once found, the bus arrival times of these bus stops will be called up based on the bus stop. 

Steps:

(1) Use the dropdown menu and select "Nearby bus stop".
(2) Give permissions for "locations".
(3) Wait for the result.
